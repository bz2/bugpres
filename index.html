Talking about bugs.

You should report bugs, well

Report as soon as you encounter the bug.

But important to get past your immediate goal having been interrupted.

As the bug report needs to make sense to other people.

Why

Sidestepping the effectiveness of getting bugs fixed for now.

  • triage
  • followup

Will we die without immediate action?

Can we effect a fix when returning later?

What

What goes into a bug report.

  • steps to reproduce
  • actual
  • expected

Three things to put in every bug report.

Also context/extra details as needed.

What version of the software, operating system, anything else noticed.

Example

One to look at.

Steps

  • Open cupboard.
  • Take jar of strawberry jam.
  • Unscrew lid of jam.

Revealing earlier mysteries.

Actual

Jam is all over the floor.

Expected

Jar is open and jam is ready to be spread.
